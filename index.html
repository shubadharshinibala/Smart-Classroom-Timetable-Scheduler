Why EduSync

Every feature your institution needs

From automated timetabling to AI conflict detection, EduSync covers the full lifecycle of academic scheduling.

Automated Timetable Generation

Generate weekly and monthly schedules in seconds. Our constraint-based engine eliminates clashes before they happen.

AI-Based Scheduling

Machine learning optimizes schedules using faculty preferences, room utilization, and subject priority scoring.

Conflict Prediction

Detect faculty clashes, room double-bookings, and time overlaps before publishing — with actionable fix suggestions.

Classroom Analytics

Track utilization rates room by room. Identify underused spaces and high-demand timeslots with rich visual reports.

Faculty Preference Scheduling

Faculty submit preferred hours and subjects. The scheduler incorporates these preferences automatically.

Attendance Integration

Mark and manage attendance directly from timetable view. Instant reports per student, subject, and department.
