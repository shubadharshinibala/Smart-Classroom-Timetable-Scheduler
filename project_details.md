#### **Smart Classroom \& Timetable Scheduler**



###### **Project Overview**



The Smart Classroom \& Timetable Scheduler is a web-based application developed to automate classroom allocation and timetable generation in educational institutions. The system helps administrators efficiently manage classrooms, faculty schedules, and course timetables while minimizing scheduling conflicts.



This project improves resource utilization, reduces manual effort, and provides a centralized platform for timetable management.



\---



###### **Problem Statement**



Traditional timetable scheduling is often performed manually, which can lead to conflicts in classroom allocation, faculty scheduling issues, and inefficient resource usage. Educational institutions require a smart and reliable system to automate timetable creation and classroom management.



The Smart Classroom \& Timetable Scheduler addresses these challenges by providing an intelligent and user-friendly scheduling solution.



\---



###### **Objectives**



\- Automate timetable generation.

\- Optimize classroom allocation.

\- Reduce scheduling conflicts.

\- Improve faculty and student timetable management.

\- Enhance resource utilization.

\- Provide real-time timetable updates.

###### **System Modules**



1\. User Management



Handles authentication and role-based access for students, faculty members, and administrators.



2\. Timetable Management



Automatically generates and manages class schedules based on predefined constraints.



3\. Classroom Allocation



Assigns available classrooms efficiently while avoiding conflicts.



4\. Faculty Management



Maintains faculty schedules and teaching assignments.



5\. Database Management



Stores timetable data, classroom information, faculty records, and course details securely.
